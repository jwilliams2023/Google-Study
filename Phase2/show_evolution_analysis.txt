Analysis of How Both Shows Have Evolved Over Time
===============================================

Survivor Evolution:
- Started with higher viewership that gradually declined over time
- Contestant count has remained relatively stable
- Locations were varied initially but settled primarily in Fiji in later seasons
- Winner demographics show diversity across age, gender, and professional backgrounds

American Idol Evolution:
- Began with massive viewership that dramatically declined
- Has gone through multiple judge configurations
- Contestant pool size has fluctuated more than Survivor
- Winner demographics skew younger with backgrounds more focused in musical fields

Comparative Evolution:
- Both shows have experienced declining viewership, reflecting broader shifts in TV consumption
- Survivor has maintained more format consistency than American Idol
- American Idol has undergone more significant production changes including network change
- Survivor has had more consistent leadership with Jeff Probst as host throughout all seasons
