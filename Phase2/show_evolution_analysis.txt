Evolution of Survivor:
1. The show started with simple survival challenges in remote locations
2. Over time, introduced more complex social dynamics and strategic gameplay
3. Added hidden immunity idols and other twists to keep the format fresh
4. Experimented with different themes (Heroes vs. Villains, Blood vs. Water)
5. Viewership peaked in early seasons and has stabilized in later years

Evolution of American Idol:
1. Started with a focus on discovering unknown talent
2. Expanded the audition process and increased drama elements
3. Changed judging panels multiple times to maintain audience interest
4. Shifted from network TV (Fox) to streaming/cable (ABC)
5. Viewership peaked in middle seasons and declined in later years
